About Me

My name is Tanner Turgeon and I am a senior at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln studying Computer Science. I started college in August of 2019 with no idea of what career I wanted to pursue. I was working two jobs at that time, one as an Evening Captain at the Legacy Estates Retirement Community and the other as a Student Worker at Love Library. With very little expectataions I began taking a multidude of exploratory courses like Astronomy, Nutrional Science, and Physics. As an undeclared major, I knew that by the time I hit my 3rd year in college it was required that I declare a major. My final semester as a Sophmore I had decided to take my first Computer Science course and that was that. I ended up loving the idea of being able to create programs and solve problems, and while debugging can be somewhat of a chore (especially after realizing it was just a missing semicolon) I was always met with a great feeling of accomplishment when finishing a project.
